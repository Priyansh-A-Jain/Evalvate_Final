Architecture decision
Use a 
Next.js Auth BFF
 (Backend-for-Frontend): all session cookies are set on the 
frontend origin
 (localhost:3000 now; app.yourdomain.com later). 
FastAPI
 remains the source of truth for credentials, JWT issuance, and user records.
This is production-ready for later deploy (same pattern works with subdomains or a reverse proxy).
